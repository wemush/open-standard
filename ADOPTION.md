## Case Studies

### Case Study 1: Mush Ohio - Farm-to-Table Traceability

**Challenge**: Customers want to know exactly where their mushrooms came from and how they were grown.

**Solution**:

- QR codes on every mushroom container
- WOLS labels encode complete cultivation history
- Customers scan to see farm, substrate, harvest date, etc.

**Results**:

- Increased customer trust
- Differentiation from competitors
- Premium pricing justified
- Regulatory compliance simplified

**Testimonial**: *"WOLS lets us prove our sustainability claims. Customers love scanning the QR code and seeing the complete story."* - Mush Ohio Team

## FAQ for Adopters

**Q: Do I have to tell you if I use WOLS?**
A: No, but we'd love to feature you! Being listed helps grow the community.

**Q: Can I use WOLS commercially?**
A: Yes! WOLS is free for commercial use under CC BY 4.0.

**Q: What if I customize WOLS?**
A: Use the `custom` namespace for extensions. Consider contributing useful additions back to the spec.

**Q: Can I get help with implementation?**
A: Yes! Join [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/wemush/open-standard/discussions) or email [support@wemush.com](mailto:support@wemush.com)

**Q: Will you promote my organization?**
A: Featured adopters get mentioned in release notes, social media, and presentations. We love sharing success stories!
